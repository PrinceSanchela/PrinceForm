Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 24, 2026

At PrinceForm, we prioritize the privacy and security of your account credentials, form designs, and form respondent submissions. This Privacy Policy details the types of information we collect, process, and protect.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following categories of data in our secure database cluster:

  • Google Account Authentication Data: When you authenticate via Google Sign-In, we receive verified account details (email address, full name, profile picture avatar) transmitted via official Google Identity Services tokens.
  • Creator Account Details: Your username, password (salted and hashed using PBKDF2 cryptography), and email address.
  • Form Configurations: Titles, descriptions, question definitions, branding themes, logos, banners, and access permission rules.
  • Form Responses & Submissions: Input answers, timestamp, and verification status submitted to forms created on PrinceForm.

2. Use of Information

The information stored inside Prince Form is used strictly to operate the features of the form builder application:

  • To authenticate your sessions, maintain dashboard operations, and authorize password resets.
  • To render your customized public form responder views to respondents.
  • To compile, structure, display, and export submission tables inside your dashboard.

3. Cookie Usage & Security

We use temporary cookie sessions (`session_token`) to keep you logged in. These cookies are configured with high-security attributes:

  • HttpOnly: Restricts browser-side script executions (anti-XSS protection).
  • SameSite (Lax): Guards against cross-site request hijacking (anti-CSRF protection).
  • Secure (HTTPS): When deployed in production, cookies are only transmitted over secure encrypted connections.

4. Data Sharing & Third-Parties

Prince Form is a self-contained open-source software project. We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third-party ad networks. All data rests in your dedicated database cluster or our secure hosted cloud server.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You have full control over your data. Deleting a form or an account in your creator dashboard immediately removes the corresponding database records from the active MongoDB database collections.
